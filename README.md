# PRODUCT-TEARDOWN — Vocallabs.ai

A research-driven product teardown of Vocallabs.ai created for the Product Intern Assignment 2026.

This project analyzes the platform from a product management perspective across:

* GTM & ICP positioning
* UX and conversion friction
* Developer ecosystem visibility
* Feature differentiation
* Analytics storytelling
* Competitive landscape

The teardown follows an **Observed → Problem → Ship Instead** framework with actionable recommendations, annotated screenshots, competitor comparisons, and prioritization based on business impact × implementation effort.

## Key Highlights

* Identified conversion blockers in the demo-gated acquisition flow
* Evaluated ICP clarity and vertical positioning strategy
* Analyzed missing self-serve product experience opportunities
* Explored developer GTM gaps around SDK/n8n integrations
* Applied strategic frameworks including Porter’s Five Forces

## Competitors Referenced

* Yellow.ai
* Retell AI
* Bolna
* VAPI
* Gnani.ai
* Synthflow AI

## Goal

The objective of this teardown is to demonstrate structured product thinking, market research, UX evaluation, and execution-oriented problem solving for modern AI SaaS platforms.
